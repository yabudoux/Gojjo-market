About Ethiopia Hotels

Welcome to Ethiopia Hotels, your premier destination for discovering and booking the finest accommodations across the beautiful country of Ethiopia. We are dedicated to connecting travelers with exceptional hotels that showcase the rich culture, stunning landscapes, and warm hospitality that Ethiopia is renowned for.

Founded with a passion for Ethiopian tourism, our platform features carefully selected hotels in major cities including Addis Ababa, Bahir Dar, Hawassa, Gondar, and Mekelle. Whether you're visiting for business, leisure, or cultural exploration, we ensure you find the perfect place to stay.

Our mission is to make hotel booking simple, secure, and tailored to your needs while supporting local businesses and promoting sustainable tourism in Ethiopia. We believe that every journey should begin with comfortable, authentic accommodations that reflect the spirit of Ethiopian hospitality.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a reservation?

Simply browse our hotels, select your preferred accommodation, choose your dates and number of guests, then complete the booking process. You'll receive a confirmation email with your booking details.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Yes, you can cancel upcoming bookings through your "My Bookings" page. Cancellation policies may vary by hotel, so please check the specific terms for your reservation.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards and local payment methods. All prices are displayed in Ethiopian Birr (ETB) for your convenience.

Do you offer group bookings?

Yes, we can assist with group bookings and special rates. Please contact us directly for groups of 10 or more rooms.
